## What I Actually Build

I care about the full path from idea to impact:

- turning messy real-world problems into reliable software
- integrating AI into products where it improves decisions and workflow
- designing backends and APIs that stay clean as projects scale
- shipping fast, then iterating with user feedback and data

I am not trying to be "someone who knows many languages".
I am trying to be an engineer who can design, build, and deliver intelligent products end-to-end.
